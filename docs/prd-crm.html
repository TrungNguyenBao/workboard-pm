CRM Product Requirements

Customer Relationship Management — Full Specification — Last updated: 2026-03-09

1. Product Overview

The CRM module in A-ERP is designed to manage the entire sales lifecycle, from lead acquisition and contact management to deal closure and post-sales tracking. It integrates deeply with Workspace and Activity Log for full auditability.

2. Product Scope

  • Contact Management: Centralized database for people (Contacts) and companies (Accounts).
  • Deal Tracking: Pipeline management with customizable stages and probability tracking.
  • Sales Funnel: Visualization of deal progression and conversion rates.
  • Activity & Tasks: Integration with PMS for follow-ups and action items.

3. Core Data Model

Entity Key Fields Description
Contact name, email, phone, title, company_id Individual person records
Account name, domain, industry, size Company/Organization records
Deal title, value, stage, probability, owner_id Sales opportunities
Lead first_name, last_name, source, status Pre-qualified contacts

4. Key Workflows

Lead Conversion

Leads are nurtured and converted into Contacts, Accounts, and Deals simultaneously. This ensures data continuity and attribution.

Deal Pipeline

Deals move through stages: Prospecting → Qualification → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost. Each transition is logged in the Activity Feed.

5. API Reference

GET    /api/v1/crm/contacts   # List contacts
POST   /api/v1/crm/deals      # Create new deal
GET    /api/v1/crm/analytics  # Sales performance data
